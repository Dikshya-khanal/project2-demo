About Nepal

Nepal is a beautiful country located in South Asia, bordered by China to the north and India to the south, east, and west. It is known for its majestic mountains, rich culture, and warm hospitality.

Famous Places

  • Mount Everest
  • Pokhara Valley
  • Lumbini
  • Kathmandu Valley
  • Chitwan National Park

Culture

Nepal is a melting pot of different cultures, religions, and traditions. The country is home to numerous ethnic groups, each with its own unique customs and practices.
