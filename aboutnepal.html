About Nepal

Discover Nepal

Nepal, a landlocked country in South Asia, is renowned for its diverse topography, ranging from the lowland plains of the Terai to the towering heights of the Himalayas. Home to Mount Everest, the world's highest peak, Nepal offers breathtaking natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and a vibrant history. The country is a melting pot of different cultures, languages, and religions, with Hinduism and Buddhism playing significant roles in shaping its cultural landscape. From the bustling streets of Kathmandu to the serene lakes of Pokhara and the wildlife-rich jungles of Chitwan, Nepal is a destination that captivates and inspires travelers from around the world.
